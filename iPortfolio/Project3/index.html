World Economic Report

Analyzing Global Economic Trends and Patterns

Project Overview

This project provides a comprehensive analysis of global economic trends and patterns using World Bank data, aiming to understand economic growth, inequality, and development across countries.

Problem Statement

  • Challenge: Analyze and understand global economic trends and disparities.
  • Objective: Identify key indicators, explore relationships, and uncover patterns in economic development.

Data Summary

The dataset encompasses indicators such as GDP, GNI, inflation, poverty rates, and education levels, aggregated for analysis.

Key Steps in the Project

  • Data Preprocessing: Cleaned and transformed datasets from the World Bank Open Data platform.
  • Analysis: Conducted exploratory data analysis, correlations, and potential predictive modeling.
  • Visualization: Created maps, charts, and dashboards to communicate findings.

Recommendations

  • Further research into specific economic phenomena.
  • Develop interactive dashboards for stakeholders.
